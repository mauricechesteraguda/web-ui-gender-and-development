GENDER RESPONSIVE ACADEME ASSESSMENT TOOL (GREA2T)

The Gender REsponsive Academe Assessment Tool (GREA2T) maps HEI functions and gender mainstreaming entry points. It is designed to assess the gender mainstreaming progress of higher education institutions in the functions of curriculum, instruction, research, and administration, with emphasis on the four gender mainstreaming entry points, namely; (1) policy; (2) people; (3) enabling mechanisms; and, (4) programs, projects and activities.

Instruction: The following statements indicate how the gender perspective is integrated or mainstreamed in instruction, research, extension, and administration vis-à-vis policy, people, enabling mechanisms, and programs, projects, and activities. Please assess the extent of gender mainstreaming in your academic institution using the following scale:

  • 5 – Very Great Extent
  • 4 – Great Extent
  • 3 – Moderate Extent
  • 2 – Least Extent
  • 1 – No extent at all
